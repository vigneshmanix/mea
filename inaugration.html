Inaugration of Mechanical Engieering Association 2013

The Inauguration of the Mechanical Engineering Association of NIT Trichy's Mechanical Engineering Department for academic year 2013-2014 was done on 15th October 2013. The program began at 9:30 a.m in the EEE Auditorium by lighting of lamp by the dignitaries followed by a prayer song by 1st year students. The welcome address was given by the Master of Ceremonies, Karthik V. M. He welcomed the dignitaries for the proceedings : The Chief Guest, Mr. D. Rajamani, Golden Rock Railway Locomotive Workshop, Dr. S. Sundarrajan the Director of NIT Trichy, Dr. J. Sarangan ,the Head of the Mechanical Engineering Department along with the President and Treasurer of the Mechanical Engineering Association.

Dr. J. Sarangan spoke about the importance of the association and how it serves as a bridge between the student community, faculty and the industries. Under the guidance of Dr. R. Anand, the MEA is expected to reach new heights. Dr. S. Sundarrajan, Director of NIT Trichy, gave an interesting speech. He wished good luck for the association and appreciated the fact that such initiatives are indeed noteworthy. He encouraged the members to form more student groups within so as to take up mini project in theoretical front and that with existing connections in industries and research and development fields, these projects can be given a practical face.

Followed by handing over mementos to the dignitaries was the Chief Guest speech Mr. Rajamani who highlighted the importance of engineers in industries and especially mechanical engineers . He put forward his views on how an association is a platform for ideas. He also gave a lecture on how the Railway workshop operates efficiently even when there is overflow of locomotives.The dignitaries were then presented with mementos and then the President of the Association described the events planned for the students of the department for the academic year. It included several workshops, Synergy, industrial visits and many more events.

One of the senior faculty members, Dr. Sankaranarayanaswamy was called upon to give an impromptu speech on the department being one of the most recognized faces of the Mechanical deparment, leading to some interesting thoughts on how the Mechanical Engineering association can be extended to other colleges and schools.

The event was concluded by Srinath Balasubramaniam, Treasurer of the Mechanical Engineering Association. He gave a vote of thanks to all the dignitaries and thanked each and every team for their support in pulling off the event in such a grand manner.The MEA had a succesful inauguration. It is bound to have some really interesting and attractive events and workshops this year.All the students are looking forward to the department’s annual symposium Synergy. The members with their enthusiasm and co-ordination will indeed take the association to great heights.

 

About Us

Mechanical engineering association is a student body organization which has been set up to provide opportunities to the students and expand their scope of learning while indulging in various extracurricular activities. it provides a platform for interactions and exchange of ideas between the professors and the students.
