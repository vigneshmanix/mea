About

Mechanical engineering association is a student body organization which has been set up to provide opportunities to the students and expand their scope of learning while indulging in various extracurricular activities. it provides a platform for interactions and exchange of ideas between the professors and the students.

Vision

To Ideate incubate and implement sound mechanical principals amongst the students.

MEA strives to

  • Act as an interface between the students and professors
  • Encourage students to take up innovative project to solve real life engineering problems
  • Conduct seminars and guest lectures
  • Provide industrial exposure through tours and visits
