Synergy 2013

Synergy is the annual national level technical symposium conducted by the students of Mechanical Engineering department. The event is usually conducted in March. It witnesses a large number of participants from various colleges in and around Trichy. Events, Workshops and Guest Lectures form the core of this symposium.

In 2013, synergy was conducted on a grand scale. It witnessed more than 2,000 participants from more than 30 different colleges. There were 4 workshops, 15 events and 3 guest lectures conducted. The total prize money to be won through the events amounted to an enormous 75,000 rupees.

Following is the list of events which were conducted in Synergy 2013:

  • Paper Presentation
  • Arresto Momentum
  • Informals
  • Fixem Up
  • Dropped
  • Project X
  • Green Aeromodelling
  • Synergy General Quiz
  • Junkyard Wars
  • Tower of Brahma
  • Flipped Logic
  • H2GO
  • How Stuff Works quiz
  • Paper Plane
  • 3D Design Event

Following is the list of workshops conducted in Synergy 2013:

  • Ornithopter Workshop
  • Design Workshop
  • Automobile Workshop
  • Motorsport Engineering Workshop

The Guest Lectures conducted in Synergy 2013 were:

  • Senior Scientist from ISRO-Evolution of Technology in ISRO, the drivers and the beneficiaries, and the state-of-the-art.
  • Prof. Gautam Biswas, Director CSIR-CMERI Durgapur-Fluid Mechanics and Heat Transfer.
  • Dr. Devesh Ranjan, Assistant Professor Texas A&M University-Conventional and Unconventional Areas of Fluid Mechanics, Heat Transfer and Combustion.

 

About Us

Mechanical engineering association is a student body organization which has been set up to provide opportunities to the students and expand their scope of learning while indulging in various extracurricular activities. it provides a platform for interactions and exchange of ideas between the professors and the students.
