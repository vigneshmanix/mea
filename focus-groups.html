Automobile Focus Group

Automobile committee is a focus group created to promote and cultivate the interest about automobile engineering among the students. This group mainly focuses on students coming up with indigenous projects related to any self propelling machine. Apart from this workshops are held to give better insights on the sound engineering principle that goes behind the working of the systems. Regular sessions are held to discuss about the modern technologies in the field of automobile and motorsports.

Aeronautics Focus Group

The Aero Committee has been formed with an intention of creating an interest group of students in the fields of Aeromodeling, Aeronautics and Aerospace. The primary objective of the group is to provide a society where enthusiasts can bring forth their ideas and queries and work together on projects. The objectives can be summarised as follows-

  • To provide a common platform where aero enthusiasts can put their ideas together.
  • To provide a place where interested students can come together and work on projects and build models.
  • To promote aeromodeling, aeronautics and aerospace in our college.
  • To apprise ourselves with fabrication techniques of RC Aircrafts and common manufacturing techniques used in Industries.
  • To go on informative trips to nearby places to get a hands-on experience in the field.

Design Focus Group

The Goal and objectives of the Design Focus group-

  • To improve the standard of designers in the Mechanical Engineering Department of NIT Trichy.
  • To participate and compete in various design competitions over the country..
  • To solve industrial design problem statements to train students to meet industry standards.

About Us

Mechanical engineering association is a student body organization which has been set up to provide opportunities to the students and expand their scope of learning while indulging in various extracurricular activities. it provides a platform for interactions and exchange of ideas between the professors and the students.
